About Veterans Remembered

Why We Exist

I tried to serve in the military, but was rejected several times due to medical reasons. I thought, "if I can't serve one way, I will serve another."

What is America, really? America, "the land of opportunity," "democracy," "freedom," and "the right to life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness." These words describe America, but have we forgotten to care for those who were willing to give their lives for us?

This is why we exist, to remind the Nation just what it means, to be a United States Veteran. Veterans were willing to lay their very lives down for us, they've been forgotten about, by the very country they defended. Now, it's our turn to stand by them.

Our Motto

"For him who hath borne the battle, that he not be forsaken."

- Brian Aspinall, Founder

America asks for the very best from our veterans, including their lives. Routinely, many are cast aside, homeless, or simply refused care. The motto of Veterans Remembered echoes President Lincoln's words, and makes our own solemn oath to veterans, "you are not forsaken, and we remember."

How You Give Back

With each purchase, money will be deposited into a separate account dedicated to service personnel or their families (veterans, fire, police, etc.) to help with any needs that can be met.

As my company grows, I will hire more veterans/service personnel to help. Your purchase will help them in so many ways.

Ordering

Next, after you have completed your order form you will be contact by Brian to determine the logistics of your order. This includes payments, shipping, design proof, etc.
